💡Tips & Reminders🌺
Little things that make a big difference 💕
💊
Retatrutide: protect your muscle!
GLP-1 triple agonists are powerful for fat loss but can cause muscle loss if protein intake drops too low. Hitting your protein target every single day is non-negotiable. Even on low-appetite days, prioritize a protein shake if you can't eat a full meal.
💧
Hydration is extra important on retatrutide
Retatrutide can blunt your thirst sensation. Set phone reminders to drink water throughout the day. Aim for at least 3L — more on gym days. Dehydration will tank your energy and gym performance fast.
🏃‍♀️
Cardio: steady state beats HIIT right now
While on a GLP-1 medication, fasted low-intensity cardio (walking, cycling at 60–70% max HR) is your fat-loss sweet spot. HIIT is great but keep it to once per week max — your recovery capacity may be lower with reduced calorie intake.
📸
The scale is a liar — take progress photos!
During recomp, you lose fat AND gain muscle simultaneously, so the scale may barely move while your body transforms completely. Take photos every 4 weeks and measure your waist — that's the real story ☕
🛁
Don't skip warm-up & cool-down
These are where adaptation actually happens. Five minutes either side of your workout is a tiny investment with a huge return — fewer injuries, better recovery, faster results.
🌷
Consistency > perfection, every time
Missed a workout? Don't double up — just pick up where you left off. One missed session changes almost nothing. Skipping every session changes everything. Show up even on the hard days 💪
💪
Week 4–6: don't quit!
The new-gym excitement fades but visible results haven't fully arrived yet. This is the critical window. Every girl who pushed through this phase is the one who actually glowed up. Trust the process 🌸
